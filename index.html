Vision & mission

Vision

Software tooling doesn't have to be heavy to be powerful. We believe the next generation of developer tools can be small, fast, and open — and that world-class engineering can come from anywhere, including Indonesia.

Mission

Build focused developer tools and the product infrastructure around them with zero-bloat engineering: measured performance, tested behavior, APIs that feel right — then open as much of it as we can.

go-routine is GO ROUTINE TECH — an independent, one-person software company from Indonesia. Small by design: the same philosophy as our tools.

How we work

  • Zero-bloat engineering. Our diagram engine has zero dependencies and ships as 166 KB of wasm. Every dependency must earn its place.
  • Proof over promise. Benchmarks in the repo, CI on every push, a public roadmap with versions on every shipped checkbox — and our own websites are rendered by our own engine.
  • One engine, many faces. Build the core once, properly; then let the CLI, desktop, web, and products share it. Features land everywhere at once.
  • Compatibility is kindness. flowmaid chases mermaid.js compatibility so people's existing diagrams just work. Clear, line-numbered errors when something doesn't yet.
